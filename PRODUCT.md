# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Hackathon judges, SOC analysts, identity defenders, and technical teammates evaluating an ITDR demo under time pressure.

## Product Purpose

AuthGraph ITDR detects Kerberoasting against Active Directory, turns telemetry into an identity incident, explains why the risk is critical, visualizes the attack path, and shows containment actions.

## Brand Personality

Precise, urgent, cinematic. The interface should feel like a serious defender workstation, not a toy dashboard.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS cards, purple AI gradients, terminal cosplay, empty cyberpunk decoration, and dashboards that require reading every label before the story is clear.

## Design Principles

- Show the attack in one glance: identity, target, path, severity, and next action must be visible immediately.
- Make evidence explainable: every critical score needs a concrete reason tied to Kerberos telemetry.
- Treat response as the climax: containment should feel decisive and show what changed.
- Work before integration: local JSON is the source of truth until backend endpoints are ready.
- Keep the interface judge-ready: beautiful, responsive, readable, and demo-stable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, and reduced-motion fallbacks for all animated effects.
